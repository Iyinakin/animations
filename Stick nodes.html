STICKNODES

Sticknodes is an android app used for making animations. It can also be used on windows using apps like Bluestacks. Sticknodes is classified into two. They are:

  • Sticknodes
  • Sticknodes Pro

Sticknodes and Sticknodes pro are almost the same. The differences between the two apps are:

There are different versions of sticknodes you can find on google.

To download stickfigures, movieclips and so on for sticknodes, go to the sticknodes website. Here you will get different stickfigures, movieclips, backgrounds, and even the latest news concerning the app like, new versions.

There are different things that you can use in animation like:

  • Stickfigures
  • Movieclips
  • Sprites and so on

MOVIECLIPS

Movieclips are small mini-animations. They can be used to avoid repetitive use of stickfigures in the animation.
